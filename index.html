Ranking System Explained

The leaderboard uses a weighted scoring system to calculate player performance across multiple metrics.

1. Leaderboard Terminology

  • KDR: Kill-to-Death Ratio (Kills ÷ Deaths)
  • FIKA: Fika COOP Mod status

2. Season System

Competitive cycles with unique leaderboards:

  • Duration: 2-3 months per season
  • Reset: New season starts when SPT major version releases
  • Hall of Fame: Top 3 players from previous season are shown permanently on the page
  • Data: Full season archives available in "Season" dropdown menu

3. Skill Formula

Final skill score is calculated using these weighted factors:

  • KDR: 20% weight
  • Survival Rate: 20% weight
  • Total Raids: 40% weight
  • PMC Level: 20% weight
  • This includes calibration:
    • Minimum 35 raids required for skill score ranking
    • 70% skill score penalty until player reaches 35 raids
    • Penalty decreases linearly until player reaches 75 raids

4. Skill Tiers

Players are classified into tiers based on final skill score:

  • L- to L+: Low (0-18)
  • M- to M+: Medium (18-21)
  • H- to H+: High (21-25)
  • P- to P+: Pro (25-30)
  • G: God (30+)

5. Visual Indicators

Statistics are color-coded based on performance thresholds:

  • Survival Rate: Bad (<30%), Average (30-54%), Good (55-64%), Impressive (65%+)
  • KDR: Poor (<5), Standard (5-12), Skilled (12-14.99), Impressive (15+)
  • Raid Duration: Short (<5m), Average (5-15m), Long (15-20m), Impressive (20m+)

Where do I find my profile?

Simply open your SPT root game folder, go to folder named 'user' and then 'profiles' folder. You'll find a .json file inside which now you can now choose with button "Open your .json SPT Profile"

What if I have more than one profile?

Open your SPT Server and SPT Launcher, log into your desired profile and see the ID under your username which will represent the name of .json file you wanna open.
